About NeuroFilter

NeuroFilter was born out of frustration with the constant distractions that modern websites throw at us. We believe that you should be in control of your digital experience, not the other way around.

Our mission is simple: help people reclaim their focus and productivity by removing the algorithmic distractions that keep us scrolling endlessly. Whether you're a student trying to research, a professional looking to stay on task, or anyone who wants to use the internet more intentionally, NeuroFilter is here to help.

Built with privacy in mind, NeuroFilter doesn't collect any data about your browsing habits. It simply does its job quietly in the background, giving you the clean, distraction-free web experience you deserve.
